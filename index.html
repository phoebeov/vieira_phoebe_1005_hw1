About My Life

My Life

Hi I'm Phoebe.
Welcome to my life.

I was born on January 25th, 19 years ago to an English woman and a Barbadian man. I've got a younger sister, three dogs and a cat. My mum started visiting Barbados annually when she was a young girl with her family and fell in love with the island. My parents had mutual friends and met at a Christmas party in their twenties. Years later they became friends, business partners and then husband and wife.

About My School

My School

I've always been a lover of school. I love to learn. Love to know things. Love to succeed. I am my biggest critic and push myself to the limit. I like to be good at things.
I first started school when I was two years old at First Steps in Barbaods, which laid the foundation for my core group of friends.
From there I moved to St. Winifred's School with my best friends from kindergarten. I spent twelve years of my academic life at that school and graduated in 2014 at the equivalent of Grade 10. After that, I packed up and moved to Canada to attend Appleby College, a boarding school in Oakville. Appleby gave me two of the greatest years of my life and I am forever grateful. Finally I moved to Western University and was enrolled in the MTP program. At first I hated my new school. It was far too big. I hated not being able to recognize people on the way to class. I hated being a unit, a nameless face within a packed out lecture hall. I was miserable. This year I couldn't be happier at Fanshawe - although I may not know all the names, I am able to recognize all the faces of the kids who spend each class with me. They understand what I'm going through, they take the same courses as I do. We have the same work loads. It works. I love that I can ask a question when I am confused, which is often. I love that my teachers know my name. I love that I have regained an identity, as opposed to being "just another student".
